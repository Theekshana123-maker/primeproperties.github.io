Description

This stunning modern family home in the heart of Beverly Hills offers luxury living at its finest. With 4 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, and approximately 2,500 square feet of living space, this property is perfect for families looking for comfort and style.

The open-concept living area features floor-to-ceiling windows that flood the space with natural light, highlighting the beautiful hardwood floors and contemporary finishes. The gourmet kitchen is a chef's dream with high-end stainless steel appliances, quartz countertops, and a spacious island with seating.

Step outside to your private oasis featuring a sparkling pool, spa, and outdoor kitchen - perfect for entertaining. The master suite includes a walk-in closet and spa-like bathroom with dual vanities and a soaking tub.

Located in one of Beverly Hills' most sought-after neighborhoods, this home is just minutes from world-class shopping, dining, and entertainment.
